NEELAN SCHOOL

NEELAN SCHOOL, celebrating 25 years of glorious service in the field of education, is a co-educational school run by the Neelan Educational Trust, founded by Thiru U. Neelan in the year 1999. Started with a humble count of 200 students in classes up to the 8th standard in the year 1999, the school today boasts more than 2200 students and nearly 100 dedicated teaching staff under the guidance of the Chairman .


OUR CAMPUS

The campus’s infrastructure is aesthetically well designed, consisting of three blocks with three floors each, with all safety norms accomplished, compromising with all necessary amenities premeditatedly towards adaptability for the upcoming changes in modern techniques in the educational system and to incorporate the best educational practises and technology to prepare students for their future endeavours. Our school was given a new facelift in 2020, enclosing three blocks for classrooms, labs, administrative rooms, and an open auditorium. The establishment encompasses all necessary infrastructures for a school, like sufficient space with natural shady trees and flowering plants, an enclosed kids' play area, and splendid, airy classrooms with a balanced curriculum and ample space for co-curricular activities, along with highly proficient faculty, which make the schooling experience a unique one, standing true to our vision of providing exceptional educational background for the best future experience and creating an empowered generation for the nation.
